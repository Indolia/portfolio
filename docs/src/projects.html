Sophos ZTNA  

Sophos ZTNA adheres to the principles of zero trust: trust nothing, verify everything. Individual users and devices form their own micro-segmented perimeters, undergoing constant validation and verification. In a zero-trust model, users are no longer automatically granted trust and access simply by being 'on the network. The solution allows for precise control over access to applications and data, relying on user identity and device health as key factors.

  • Active contributor to iOS app
  • Authored PRDs and PoCs for new features
  • Reviewed Pull Requests and mentored peers
  • Collaborated closely with cross-functional teams

Technologies

Swift, Objective-C, CoreData, System Extensions, XPC, WebSocket
